The Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan is a system of premier central government schools in India

that are instituted under the aegis of the Ministry of Human Resource Development,

Government Of India.

It has a total of 1225 schools in India and three abroad.

It is one of the world's largest chains of schools.

Headquarters: New Delhi

Founded: 15 December 1963

Motto: Tattvaṁ pūṣaṇa apāvr̥ṇu

Commissioner: Mr. S. K Mall, IAS

School board: Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE)

Authority: Ministry of Human Resource Development
